Posture Tips

  • Keep your head balanced over your shoulders
  • Position your monitor at eye level
  • Keep shoulders relaxed and back
  • Take short breaks every hour
  • Sit with your back straight against the chair
  • Keep feet flat on the floor or on a footrest

About Ducktor

Ducktor is your friendly posture companion that helps you maintain good posture while working at your computer.

Using your webcam, Ducktor monitors your posture in real-time and provides feedback when it detects issues. Our favorite duck will quack at you when it's time to straighten up!

Regular breaks are important too, so Ducktor can remind you to take short breaks throughout your day.
